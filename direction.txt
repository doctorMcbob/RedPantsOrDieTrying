basic layout, completely subject to change

small opening level
  . just platforms and spikes
  . to teach the basics of platforming

main section
  . start in middle
  . explore outward
  . each biome has a specific collectable
     - gems in the cave, fruit in the jungle... ect
  . each biome has a boss door needing some number of collectables
     - maybe like 80% of the total
  . each biome has different actors and mechanics
  . 5(?) biomes
     - cave   : jems
     - jungle : fruit
     - bank   : coins
     - ...
  . once all bosses are defeated path to the final level opens

final stretch
  . more or less linear
  . has actors from all biomes
  . ends with final boss
